# Code of Conduct

Multi-Agent Memory Isolation is built for calm, evidence-first collaboration.

## Expected

- Be respectful.
- Assume good faith.
- Keep discussions specific and actionable.
- Bring evidence when reporting behavior or design problems.
- Protect private data.

## Not Accepted

- Harassment or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information.
- Secret leaks, credential dumps, or doxxing.
- Repeated bad-faith disruption.

Maintainers may remove content or restrict participation when needed to keep the project useful and safe.
